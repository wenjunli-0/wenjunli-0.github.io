About
I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Singapore Management University, supervised by Professor Pradeep Varakantham. My research interests lie in the field of how to train RL agents and humans to be generalizable and make the training more effectively and efficiently. For an example of my recent work, see UTSD.

Before joining SMU, I was a MSc student in Eletrical Engineering at Viterbi Engineering School, University of Souther California, where I discovered the joys of machine learning and AI research. Aside from AI research, I also enjoy travelling and supporting FC Bayern Munich.
